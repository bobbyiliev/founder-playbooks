AI music generation has reached the point where the output is genuinely good. But nobody has yet taken the obvious next step: creating a full artist persona, building a fanbase, and releasing music as a branded AI act. The opportunity isn't just in the music — it's in the merch, the live shows (with visuals), the social media presence, and the content machine you can build around a character that never needs sleep.
