Sheel used AI tools to blast through a real estate licensing course in a fraction of the normal time. The license itself unlocked access to MLS data and commission savings on his own transactions. The tactic: identify certifications or licenses where the knowledge barrier has been lowered by AI, but the credential still carries real economic value.
