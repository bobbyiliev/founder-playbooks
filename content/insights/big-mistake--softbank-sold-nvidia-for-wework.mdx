SoftBank held a significant stake in Nvidia in its early days. They sold it to free up capital for other Vision Fund bets — including their massive investment in WeWork. The Nvidia stake alone would have been worth more than the entire Vision Fund's returns. The lesson: conviction in the wrong thing at scale doesn't become wisdom. It becomes a very expensive mistake.
