Founders love building systems. CRMs, dashboards, automations, SOPs. But Pat makes a critical distinction: systems manage complexity, they don't create growth. If you're not growing, no amount of systematization will fix it. You need to do the uncomfortable thing — talk to customers, make the sales calls, ship the feature. Systems are for when things are working and you need to handle the volume. They're not a substitute for traction.
