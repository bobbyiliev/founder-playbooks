The original iPhone app gold rush died because build costs rose and the App Store became saturated. But two things have changed: AI has collapsed the cost of building an app to nearly zero, and TikTok has created a new distribution channel that bypasses the App Store's discovery problem entirely. Pat argues we're in a second gold rush — simple, focused apps that solve one problem and market through short-form video. The barrier to entry is lower than it's been in a decade.
