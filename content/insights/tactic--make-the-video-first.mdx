Before writing a single line of code, make a TikTok video showing what the app would do. If the video gets traction, build the app. If it doesn't, you just saved yourself weeks of development time. Pat's insight: TikTok's algorithm gives you an honest signal about market demand faster than any other method. The video is your MVP, not the app.
