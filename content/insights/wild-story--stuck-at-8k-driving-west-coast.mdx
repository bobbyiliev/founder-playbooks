Pat Walls tells the story of a guy making $8K/month from a simple app while living with his mom, convinced he was failing. He kept chasing new ideas instead of doubling down on the one that was already generating real revenue. He drove up and down the West Coast looking for inspiration when the answer was sitting in his app dashboard the whole time. The lesson: sometimes the thing that feels too small or too boring is exactly the thing you should go all-in on.
