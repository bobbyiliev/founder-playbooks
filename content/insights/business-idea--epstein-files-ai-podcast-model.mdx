When the Epstein files dropped, someone quickly created an AI-generated podcast that summarized and discussed the documents. It went viral. Shaan sees this as a repeatable model: any time a large corpus of dense, public material appears — court documents, government reports, leaked archives — you can spin up an AI podcast that makes it accessible. The production cost is near zero, the speed-to-market advantage is massive, and the audience is pre-built around the topic.
