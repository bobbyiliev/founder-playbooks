The boutique fitness market is massive but almost entirely focused on 25-45 year olds. Meanwhile, the 55+ demographic has more disposable income, more free time, and a greater health motivation than any other age group. Sheel sees a gap: a premium, community-driven fitness experience designed specifically for older adults. Think Barry's energy and brand quality, but with programming, pacing, and community tailored to people over 55.
